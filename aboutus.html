About AYSA Kent

Empowering African youth through sports and education in South King County

African Youth Sports Organization

We are an African organization that offers sports programs to African youth ages 2 to 16 throughout South King County - serving Kent, Tukwila, SeaTac, Renton, and surrounding communities. AYSA Kent Academy is a branch of African Youth Sports that does community work and plans sporting events.

Our Headquarters:

20804 International Blvd
SeaTac, WA 98198

Our Story

AYSA Kent was founded in 2015 with the vision of creating a safe space for African youth to develop athletic skills while building character and community. What began as a small soccer program has grown into a comprehensive sports academy serving over 500 youth annually throughout South King County.

Our founder, Ali Abdulla, grew up in the area and understands the challenges local African youth face. He created AYSA to provide positive mentorship and opportunities through sports, with programs specifically designed for children ages 2-16.

We Serve:
Kent Tukwila SeaTac Renton Federal Way

Our Mission

To empower African youth through sports by developing athletic skills, building character, and fostering academic achievement in a supportive community environment throughout South King County.

Our Vision

A community where every African youth in South King County has access to quality sports programming that helps them develop into confident, capable leaders both on and off the field.

Our Values

Respect • Teamwork • Integrity • Accountability • Excellence • Community • Cultural Pride
